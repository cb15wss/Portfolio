A little about me

Hie There! I'm Christine Banda from Manchester England United Kingdom.

After working in health and social care for about 8 years, I decided to add more skills to my portfolio. Thats when I enrolled at the University of Bolton on a 2 year course of Foundation Degree in health and social care.

After a year into the course I found myself spending most of my free time in Tech and fell in love with it. I decided to change my Career path to Computing at the University and changed my course to Computging and Web Development which was a 4 year course as I had to do a Foundation year due to change of path.

I took a gap year in 2016 when I had my daughter and went back to Bolton University to finish my course in September 2017 when my daughter was just 6 months old

I was nominated for an outstanding Student award by the University after showing my hard work and being at the University earning high grades while raising my 4 Children while the youngest was just 2 at the time.

I won a silver Price which I received at The WCIT’s 103rd Business Lunch on the 20th February, held in the stunning Wax Chandlers’ Hall in the City of London. On the left is a picture of me receiving a WCIT outstanding Student Award .

I graduated at the University of Bolton in July 2019 with a First Class Honors Degree in computing and website Development.

Since then, I joined NorthCoders Manchester Coding Bootcamp where I have earned more skills enough to become a full stack JavaScript software Engineer.
